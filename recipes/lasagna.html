Lasagna

Ultimate Lasagna Bolgnese

Rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel and loads of mozzarella make up this ultimate lasagna

Ingredients

Meat

  • 34 oz Ground beef or you could use a mix of ground pork and beef

Produce

  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Celery
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Parsley or basil, fresh
  • 24 oz Passata or strained tomatoes
  • 14 oz Tomatoes

Canned Goods

  • 2 Beef or vegetable bouillon cubes
  • 3 heaping tbsp Tomato paste

Condiments

  • 1 tsp Sugar or honey
  • tsp Worcestershire sauce

Pasta & Grains

  • 3 oz Lasagna, fresh

Baking & Spices

  • 1/4 cup All purpose flour
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning blend
  • 1 Pinch Nutmeg or clove
  • 2 Salt and pepper

Oils & Vinegar

  • 2 tbsp Olive oil

Dairy

  • 4 tbsp Butter
  • 17 oz Mozzarella cheese, fresh
  • cup Parmesan, fresh grated

Beer, Wine, Liquor

  • 1/4 cup Red wine

Other

  • 3½ cups while milk room temperature or warmed slightly
