Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Grilled Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

A childhood classic sandwich you didn't know could get any better. Melty, warm peanut butter and sweet jelly are perfect with a glass of cold milk.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp Jelly or jam
  • 8 tsp Land o lakes cinnamon sugar butter spread
  • 8 tbsp Peanut butter, creamy
  • 8 slices Wheat sandwich bread, hearty

Steps

  1. For each sandwich, spread 2 tablespoons peanut butter onto 1 slice of bread. Spread 1 tablespoon jelly onto another slice of bread. Place on top of peanut butter, jelly-side down. Spread 1 teaspoon butter onto outside of each slice of bread.
  2. Heat 12-inch skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  3. Place 2 sandwiches into skillet. Cook, turning once, 4-6 minutes or until golden brown and peanut butter is melted. Repeat with remaining sandwiches.
